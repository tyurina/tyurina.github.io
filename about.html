More About Me

Hi, I'm Tyurina. Currently I'm a 2A Mechatronics Engineering Student at the University of Waterloo. I'm hardworking and creative individual always looking to learn new things.

Why Mechatronics ?

Robots, Robots, Robots! On a more serious note, I love the versatility of the program and the collabaration of historically seperated disciplines. In our world, systems are not simply electrical or mechanical in nature; rather they are a fusion or "multi-disciplinary". Mechatronics offers great exposure to the multi-disciplinary technology of our world while giving individauls oppurtunity to specialize in the areas they are most intersted in.

Great Mechatronics, what next ?

That's the best part! I am a learning enthusiast interested in aerial robotics, bionic systems, innovative software and cutting edge technology. I am still exploring all the different niches available in this diverse industry, learning more and more each day. I think everything is a valuable learning oppurtunity and want to develop a wide pallette of technical and practical skills.
